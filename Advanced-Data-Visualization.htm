Advanced Data Visualization:¶

Below is a display of subplots visualizing the stats and characteristics of different pokemon. The idea was to look for trends in the data to better understand the game and how the characteristics of pokemon play into their total stats and therefore, their strength.

import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import seaborn as sns

#Displaying boxplot of total per type:
plt.subplot(2, 2, 1)
sns.boxplot(x=pokemon['Type 1'], y=pokemon['Total'], data=pokemon)
plt.xticks(rotation=60)
plt.title('Total Stat Points per First Type')

#Displaying boxplot of total per generation:
plt.subplot(2, 2, 2)
sns.boxplot(x=pokemon['Generation'], y=pokemon['Total'], data=pokemon)
plt.title('Total Stat Points per Generation')


#Displaying scatter plot of physical vs special attack and defense:
plt.subplot(2, 2, 3)
plt.scatter(x=pokemon['Attack'], y=pokemon['Sp. Atk'], marker='o', color='black', label='Attack', s=15)
plt.scatter(x=pokemon['Defense'], y=pokemon['Sp. Def'], marker='s', color='firebrick', label='Defense', s=10)
plt.ylabel('Special')
plt.xlabel('Physical')
plt.legend(loc='upper right')
plt.title('Attack vs. Defense')

#Displaying bar plot of average total stat points of legedaries vs. non-legendaries:
plt.subplot(2, 2, 4)
leg_status = ['Non-Legendary', 'Legendary']
avg_total = pokemon.groupby('Legendary')['Total'].mean()
plt.bar(leg_status, avg_total, color=['firebrick', 'g'])
plt.ylabel('Total')
plt.xlabel('Legendary Status')
plt.title('Total Stat Points for Legendaries vs. Non-Legendaries')

#Cleaning up the subplots and showing them:
plt.tight_layout()
plt.show()

The first subplot allows us to see that certain types of pokemon may be more powerful than others, such as dragon type. The second subplot was created to determine if certain generations of pokemon may be stronger than others. The visualization does not indicate as much. The third plot was used to determine if their may be a positive relationship between special and physical stats, which there appears to be. The final plot aimed to parse out whether legendaries are truly stronger than non-legendaries, and their higher average total stat points would indicate that this is likely true.
